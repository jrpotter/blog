    Common.Geometry.Rectangle.Skew #

    A characterization of a skew rectangle.

    source
    ink
    structure Geometry.Rectangle.Skew :
    Type
    • tl : Geometry.Point
    • bl : Geometry.Point
    • br : Geometry.Point
    • has_right_angle : Geometry.Point.angle tl bl br = Real.pi / 2

    A Skew rectangle is characterized by three points and the angle formed between them.

    A Point can alternatively be viewed as a Skew rectangle in which all three points coincide. A Segment can alternatively be viewed as a Skew rectangle in which two of the three points coincide. Note that, by definition of Point.angle, such a situation does indeed yield an angle of π / 2.
